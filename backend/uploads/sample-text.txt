Chapter 1: The Beginning

Once upon a time, in a small village nestled between rolling hills and ancient forests, there lived a young woman named Sarah. She had always been fascinated by the stories her grandmother told about the magical creatures that supposedly dwelled in the nearby woods.

Sarah worked as a librarian in the village library, a cozy building filled with countless books and the whispered dreams of generations. Every morning, she would unlock the heavy wooden doors and breathe in the familiar scent of old paper and leather bindings.

One particular autumn morning, as golden leaves danced outside the tall windows, Sarah discovered something extraordinary. Hidden behind a row of dusty encyclopedias was a book she had never seen before. Its cover was made of deep blue leather, embossed with silver symbols that seemed to shimmer in the lamplight.

As she opened the mysterious book, the symbols began to glow softly, and Sarah felt a strange tingling sensation in her fingertips. The first page contained only a single line of text: "For those who believe in magic, magic believes in them."
